InfoSnare
Created by: Biscuit (LowHypenE) and contributors

Purpose:
For ethical simulation and cybersecurity education in controlled environments.

Development & Support:
- System data capture and formatting by Biscuit
- Educational refactor and structure by OpenAI Assistant

Libraries Used:
- psutil: System usage statistics
- pyperclip: Clipboard access
- opencv-python: Webcam integration
- pillow: Image manipulation
- wmi: Windows Management Instrumentation
- requests: (legacy, not used in InfoSnare)

Legal & Ethical Reminder:
Use only in lab or with full consent. This project is for learning and blue team defense simulation.

Thank you for using InfoSnare!
